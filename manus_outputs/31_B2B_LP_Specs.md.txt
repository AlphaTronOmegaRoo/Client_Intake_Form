### Product Requirements Document: AgentSphere Landing Page

#### Executive Overview

* **Product:** AgentSphere: Autonomous AI Agent Platform
* **Purpose:** Convert solopreneurs and small business owners (like our "Alex Chen" avatar) by demonstrating a clear, no-code path to automating and monetizing their digital projects. The page must build trust, showcase the "mission control" value prop, and drive signups for a free trial.

---

#### Page Architecture & UI Components

**1. Navigation Bar**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Position:** Fixed/sticky header, remains visible on scroll.
    * **Components:**
        * Logo (left-aligned, clickable to home).
        * Primary navigation links: `Features`, `Use Cases`, `Pricing`, `Blog`.
        * Secondary actions: `Start Free Trial` (primary CTA button), `Sign In` (text link).
    * **Behavior:**
        * Desktop: Horizontal navigation.
        * Mobile: Hamburger menu with slide-out drawer.
        * Transparent on hero, solid background (`Midnight Blue`) after scroll.
    * **Specifications:**
        * Height: 72px desktop, 60px mobile.
        * Max-width: 1400px, centered.
        * Z-index: 1000.

**2. Hero Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Layout:** Two-column split (55/45) - Copy left, Visual right.
    * **Copy Structure:**
        * **Pre-headline:** Small text badge (pill-shaped). Example: "Trusted by 1,000+ Solopreneurs & Creators".
        * **Headline (H1):** `Stop Being the Bottleneck. Start Being the Architect.`
        * **Subheadline:** `AgentSphere is a no-code platform for building, deploying, and managing a "digital workforce" of AI agents. Automate your Etsy store, your content channels, and your social media from one dashboard.`
        * **Trust Indicators:** Logo bar: `Shopify`, `Etsy`, `Pinterest`, `YouTube`, `OpenAI`. Grayscale with hover color.
        * **Dual CTA Strategy:**
            * Primary: `Start Your Free Trial` (high-contrast button, `MagicUI` Shiny Button).
            * Secondary: `Watch 2-min Demo` (outline/ghost button).
        * **Micro-copy:** "14-day free trial • No credit card required • Cancel anytime".
    * **Visual Component:**
        * **Type:** Animated product screenshot of the "Monetization Dashboard".
        * **Requirements:** Shows agent status (active, pending), real-time revenue graphs, and the "Project Pipeline Manager". Subtle loop (15-20 sec) showing tasks completing.
        * **Technical:** `MagicUI` Blur-In on load.

**3. Problem/Pain Point Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Purpose:** Resonate deeply with the "Problem Aware" (Alex) and "Unaware" (Sarah) avatars.
    * **Headline (H2):** `Are You Drowning in Digital 'Busywork'?`
    * **Problem Statements:** 3-column grid (CSS Grid).
        * **Column 1: "Stagnant Growth"**
            * Icon: `[Icon of a trapped brain/idea]`
            * Text: "Your best ideas (Etsy stores, YouTube channels, blogs) are trapped in your notebook because you're swamped with manual uploads, scheduling, and admin."
        * **Column 2: "Tool Overload"**
            * Icon: `[Icon of tangled wires/apps]`
            * Text: "You're paying for 5+ different tools (Zapier, Make, Schedulers, AI writers) that don't talk to each other, creating a 'spaghetti' workflow that breaks."
        * **Column 3: "No Clear ROI"**
            * Icon: `[Icon of a question mark on a graph]`
            * Text: "You're 'automating' with Zaps, but have no idea if your efforts are actually saving time or making you money. You're flying blind."
    * **Supporting Stat:** "The average solopreneur spends 20+ hours per week on non-billable admin tasks. - *Solopreneur Today*"
    * **Technical:** Contrasting background (`Light Gray`).

**4. Solution Positioning Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Purpose:** Introduce AgentSphere as the "mission control" Alex was looking for.
    * **Headline (H2):** `One Dashboard to Build, Manage, and Monetize Your Digital Workforce.`
    * **Solution Narrative:**
        * "Stop gluing tools together. AgentSphere isn't *another* tool—it's your new digital team. We provide a single 'mission control' where you can build, deploy, and monitor autonomous AI agents to run your business *for* you."
        * "From your 'Agent Builder,' you give simple English commands. The agent executes the entire workflow. On your 'Monetization Dashboard,' you watch the ROI in real-time."
    * **Visual Proof:**
        * A "Before" vs. "After" diagram.
        * **Before:** A chaotic diagram showing icons for `Zapier`, `Sheets`, `Etsy`, `Pinterest`, `OpenAI`, all connected with tangled red arrows. Labeled "Spaghetti Automation."
        * **After:** Icons for `Etsy`, `Pinterest`, `OpenAI` all connecting to a central `AgentSphere` logo, which outputs to a clean graph. Labeled "Agent-Led Automation."

**5. Features & Benefits Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Structure:** Alternating layout (image-text) for our 3 core features.
    * **Feature 1: No-Code "Agent Builder"**
        * **Text (left):** (H3) `Build Agents in Plain English.` (Desc) "Stop fighting with APIs and webhooks. Our builder lets you define complex workflows with simple commands. 'When a file is added to this folder, use AI to write a title, 5 tags, and a description. Then, create a new draft listing on Etsy.'"
        * **Visual (right):** Screenshot of the Agent Builder UI, showing the text prompt and the resulting "workflow" blocks.
    * **Feature 2: Unified "Monetization Dashboard"**
        * **Text (right):** (H3) `Track Your ROI, Not Just Your Tasks.` (Desc) "Finally, a single source of truth. See exactly how much revenue each agent is generating, what its costs are (e.g., API credits), and its real-time profitability. Know what's working, instantly."
        * **Visual (left):** Close-up screenshot of the dashboard widgets, showing `[Agent: EtsyBot | Revenue: $1,450 | Cost: $42 | ROI: 3,450%]`.
    * **Feature 3: Project Pipeline Manager**
        * **Text (left):** (H3) `Automate, then Approve.` (Desc) "Put your approval on autopilot. Have agents generate 100 blog post ideas or 50 t-shirt designs and send them to your 'Pending' pipeline. You just click 'Approve' to deploy, or 'Reject' to discard."
        * **Visual (right):** Screenshot of the Kanban-style "Project Pipeline Manager" with cards for "Pending," "Approved," and "Rejected."

**6. ROI Calculator Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Purpose:** Provide a tangible "Aha!" moment for the "Problem Aware" avatar.
    * **Headline:** `How Much Time and Revenue Are You Leaving on the Table?`
    * **Input Fields:**
        * `Hours spent on manual tasks per week` (Slider: 0-40)
        * `Your estimated hourly rate` (Input: $XX)
        * `New projects you *haven't* launched` (Slider: 0-10)
    * **Calculation Logic:**
        * `Time Saved per Year` = (Hours/week * 52) * 0.8 (80% saved)
        * `Value of Time Saved` = (Time Saved) * (Hourly Rate)
        * `Estimated New Revenue` = (New Projects * $1,500/mo) * 12 (based on avatar desire)
    * **Output Display:**
        * Large Number: `Estimated Annual Value: $XX,XXX`
        * Breakdown: "Time Savings: $X,XXX" + "New Revenue: $XX,XXX"
    * **CTA:** `Start Your Trial & Claim This Value`

**7. Social Proof / Case Study Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Structure:** Featured case study + 3-column testimonial grid.
    * **Primary Case Study Component:**
        * **Layout:** Full-width feature with logo, quote, and metrics.
        * **Avatar:** `Alex Chen, Designer & E-commerce Owner`
        * **Key Results:** `30+ Hours/Week Saved` | `5 New Automated Stores` | `$4,200+ New Monthly Revenue`
        * **Quote:** `"I'm not a digital janitor anymore. I'm an architect. My business is finally growing while I sleep, and I'm free to do what I actually love—create."`
    * **Supporting Testimonials (Grid):**
        * **Card 1 (Sarah):** "I finally feel like a real professional. AgentSphere automates all my Pinterest and blog posts. It's the partner I always needed." - *Sarah J., Food Blogger*
        * **Card 2 (David):** "This is a scaling machine. I launched 10 test stores in one weekend. The dashboard shows me which ones are winners. Insane." - *David, E-com Grinder*
        * **Card 3 (Maria):** "I automated my entire client reporting process. It saves my agency 40 hours a month. It's a no-brainer." - *Maria G., Agency Owner*

**8. Pricing Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Structure:** 3-tier pricing table. Monthly/Annual toggle.
    * **Tier 1: `Hobbyist`** (For Sarah)
        * `$19/mo`
        * `3 Agents` | `5,000 Tasks/mo`
        * Core Features, Standard Integrations
        * CTA: `Start Free Trial`
    * **Tier 2: `Solopreneur`** (For Alex)
        * `$49/mo` - **(Highlighted "Most Popular")**
        * `15 Agents` | `25,000 Tasks/mo`
        * Everything in Hobbyist + `Project Pipeline`
        * CTA: `Start Free Trial` (Primary Button Style)
    * **Tier 3: `Agency`** (For Maria)
        * `$149/mo`
        * `Unlimited Agents` | `100,000 Tasks/mo`
        * Team Members, Client Dashboards, Priority Support
        * CTA: `Start Free Trial`
    * **FAQ Micro-Section:** "Can I change plans?" "What counts as a 'task'?" "What's included in the trial?"

**9. Integration & Security Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Structure:** Two sub-sections side by side.
    * **Integrations Component:**
        * **Headline:** `Connects With the Tools You Already Use`
        * **Logo Grid:** `Shopify`, `Etsy`, `Pinterest`, `YouTube`, `Instagram`, `Google Drive`, `Notion`, `Slack`, `OpenAI`, `Midjourney`, `ElevenLabs`, `WooCommerce`.
        * **CTA:** `View All Integrations`
    * **Security & Compliance Component:**
        * **Headline:** `Pro-Grade Security & Reliability`
        * **Badges:** `SOC 2 Type II`, `GDPR Compliant`
        * **Feature List:** `Secure API Key Storage (Encrypted)`, `256-bit Encryption`, `99.9% Uptime`, `Role-Based Access Control` (for Agency plan).
        * **Link:** `View Security Documentation`

**10. Demo/Video Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Headline:** `See AgentSphere in Action (in 2 Minutes)`
    * **Video Player:** Embedded video (Vimeo).
    * **Content:** A 2-minute walkthrough showing the *exact* "Alex Chen" workflow: `[File Drag-and-Drop]` -> `[Agent Builder UI]` -> `[Etsy Listing Appears]` -> `[Monetization Dashboard Updates]`.
    * **Secondary CTA:** `Ready to build yours? Start Your Free Trial.`

**11. Final CTA Section**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Headline:** `Ready to Stop 'Doing' and Start 'Directing'?`
    * **Supporting Text:** "Join 1,000+ solopreneurs, creators, and agencies using AgentSphere to automate their work and scale their ideas. Start your 14-day free trial today."
    * **Dual CTAs:**
        * Primary: `Start Free Trial` (Large button).
        * Secondary: `Watch 2-min Demo` (Outline button).
    * **Trust Elements:** "14-day trial" "No credit card required" "Cancel anytime"
    * **Visual Design:** Full-width section using the primary brand gradient: `linear-gradient(90deg, #00C9FF 0%, #92FE9D 100%)`.

**12. Footer**

* **Requirements:**
    * **Structure:** 4-column layout.
    * **Column 1: Company:** Logo, Tagline, Social Icons.
    * **Column 2: Platform:** `Features`, `Integrations`, `Pricing`, `Security`.
    * **Column 3: Use Cases:** `For E-commerce`, `For Content Creators`, `For Bloggers`, `For Agencies`.
    * **Column 4: Resources:** `Blog`, `Help Center`, `API Documentation`, `Contact`.
    * **Legal:** (Below columns) Copyright, `Privacy Policy`, `Terms of Service`.

#### Visual Style Guide
*(This section is populated **directly** from our `2_brand.md` document)*

**Color Palette**

* **Primary Brand Colors:**
    * Gradient: `linear-gradient(90deg, #00C9FF 0%, #92FE9D 100%)`
    * `#00C9FF` (Deep Sky Blue)
    * `#20D4F5` (Cyan)
    * `#40E0EC` (Turquoise)
    * `#60EBE2` (Aqua)
    * `#80F7D9` (Mint)
    * `#92FE9D` (Spring Green)
* **Neutral Colors:**
    * `#0E1a35` (Midnight Blue - Dark Backgrounds, Primary Text)
    * `#8A94A6` (Medium Gray - Secondary Text)
    * `#F3F4F6` (Light Gray - Backgrounds)
    * `#FFFFFF` (White - Primary Backgrounds)
    * `#111827` (Black - Dark Mode Text)
* **Semantic Colors:**
    * Success: `#22C55E`
    * Warning: `#F59E0B`
    * Error: `#EF4444`
    * Info: `#3B82F6`

**Typography**

* **Font Families:**
    * Headings (H1, H2, H3): `DM Serif Display`, serif
    * Body & UI (H4-H6, Body, Small): `Inter`, sans-serif
* **Type Scale:**
| Element | rem | px | line-height | Font |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Display** | 4.5rem | 72px | 1.1 | `DM Serif Display` |
| **H1** | 3rem | 48px | 1.2 | `DM Serif Display` |
| **H2** | 2.25rem | 36px | 1.25 | `DM Serif Display` |
| **H3** | 1.875rem | 30px | 1.3 | `DM Serif Display` |
| **H4** | 1.5rem | 24px | 1.4 | `Inter` |
| **H5** | 1.25rem | 20px | 1.5 | `Inter` |
| **H6** | 1.125rem | 18px | 1.5 | `Inter` |
| **Body (Regular)** | 1rem | 16px | 1.6 | `Inter` |
| **Body (Small)** | 0.875rem | 14px | 1.5 | `Inter` |
| **Body (XSmall)**| 0.75rem | 12px | 1.4 | `Inter` |
* **Font Weights:** `Inter` (300, 400, 500, 600), `DM Serif Display` (400, 700 - if available, else 400)

**Spacing System**

* Base Unit: 8px
* Scale: `0.25rem` (4px), `0.5rem` (8px), `1rem` (16px), `1.5rem` (24px), `2rem` (32px), `3rem` (48px), `4rem` (64px).
* Section Padding: `5rem` (80px) top/bottom.

**Component Styles**

* **Buttons:**
    * Primary: Background (Brand Gradient), Text (White), `border-radius: 1rem` (from `2_brand.md`), Padding: `16px 32px`, font-weight: 600.
    * Secondary: Background (Transparent), Border: `2px solid #00C9FF`, Text (`#00C9FF`), `border-radius: 1rem`.
* **Cards:**
    * Background: White (light), `#0E1a35` (dark).
    * Border: `1px solid #F3F4F6` (light), `#111827` (dark).
    * `border-radius: 1rem` (from `2_brand.md`).
    * Box-shadow: `0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`.
* **Input Fields:**
    * Height: 48px, `border-radius: 0.5rem`, Border: `1px solid #F3F4F6`.
    * Focus: Border color `(Deep Sky Blue, #00C9FF)`.

**Iconography:**

* **Style:** `Heroicons` (Line, 2px stroke width).
* **Size:** 24px standard, 32px for feature icons.

**Animation & Interactions**

* **Libraries:** `Framer Motion` (complex), `MagicUI` (hero/marketing).
* **Scroll Animations:** `MagicUI` `Blur-In` on section load.
* **Button Hover:** `MagicUI` `Shiny Button` effect for primary CTAs.

**Responsive Breakpoints**

* `sm`: 640px
* `md`: 768px (Tablet - stack features)
* `lg`: 1024px (Desktop)
* `xl`: 1280px (Container max-width 1200px)
* `2xl`: 1536px (Container max-width 1400px)

**Technical Requirements**

* **Performance:** Lighthouse > 90. Lazy load images (`WebP`) and video.
* **Code Architecture:** React/Next.js, Tailwind CSS, DaisyUI.
* **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA. All `Inter` text must pass contrast ratios. `DM Serif Display` is for large headlines only.
* **Analytics:** Event tracking for "Start Free Trial" clicks, "Demo" clicks, and "ROI Calculator" submissions.
