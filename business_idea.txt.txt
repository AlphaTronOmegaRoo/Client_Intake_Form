App Name: AgentSphere
Who it's for: Solopreneurs and small businesses who want to leverage automated, monetizable projects.
What problem it solves: It solves the difficulty of deploying, managing, and tracking the profitability of multiple autonomous AI agents (e.g., agents running Etsy stores, automated content channels, or social media).
3 Main Features: A no-code "Agent Builder" to define tasks and goals, a unified "Monetization Dashboard" that tracks all agent activities and revenue, and a "Project Pipeline Manager" to approve or pause agent-generated projects.
